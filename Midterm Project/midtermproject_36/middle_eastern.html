Middle Eastern Cuisine

Some of our favorite dishes from Middle Eastern cuisine.

Chicken Kebab

Chicken kebabs are cubes of chicken marinated in a blend of herbs and spices, skewered, then grilled.

Falafel

Falafel is a deep fried ball of chickpeas and other herbs and spices.

Shakshuka

Shakshuka is a dish with poached eggs in a tomato based sauce. Served with pita bread.

Pilaf

Pilaf is a rice dish where the rice is cooked in a stock or a broth along with other aromatics.

Baklava

Baklava is a pastry made with filo dough filled with nuts and held together with honey.

Hummus With Vegetables

Hummus is a chickpea paste along with other flavorings, served with vegetables to dip it in.
